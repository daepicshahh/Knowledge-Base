Fast Fashion: What it is, it's impact and more.

You may be wondering-- what even is Fast Fashion. Here's some of my current notes of Fast Fashion, written below.

Impact on environment:

  • Just a single t-shirt takes up roughly 2700 litres of water. Manufacturing a simple cotton t-shirt uses harmful pesticides which hurts animals living around them, farmers because they breath in these gases and pollutes the air even further.
  • Approximately 92 million tonnes of textile waste is produced anually throughout the world. The fashion industry is repsonsible for roughly 35% of the world ocean microplastic pollution.
